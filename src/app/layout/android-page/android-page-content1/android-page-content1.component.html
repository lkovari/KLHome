AE. Mobile application
This application is the mobile client for AmkaiSolution, Amkai Office business solution for health care. Together with my colleague Peter D. we developed the AE.Mobile v.1.0 mobile client application (and it's server side) in our spare time, mainly at nights. After finishing the v1.0 it was presented to the TCO. of Amkai LLC. and took an offer to them. The TCO. told us that the company would not not use it, so the product will not made from this mobile application as it doesn't fit into their business plan. The users of the AE. who have accounted for the AmkaiEnterprise software can be logged in securely into mobile client software with their own login name and password, what is usually used in the AmkaiEnterprise. When logged in into mobile application, it shows the current daily scheduling data for the logged in staff member or surgeon, and it shows a list of the original color of each appointments. The mobile client gets the scheduling data through a special web service which is not the part of the AmkaiEnterprise software.
