AE. Mobile application
This application is the mobile client for Solution, Office business solution for health care Together with my colleague Peter Dudás, we developed the AE.Mobile v.1.0 mobile client application (and it's server side) in our spare time, mainly at nights. After finishing the v1.0 it was presented to the TCO. of the LLC. and took an offer to them. The TCO. told us that the company would not use it, so the product will not made from this mobile application as it didn't fit into their business plan. The users of the AE. who have accounted for the Enterprise software can be logged in securely into mobile client software, with their own login name and password, what is used in the Enterprise. When the user logged in, into mobile application, it shows the current daily scheduling data for the logged in staff member or surgeon, and it shows a list of the original color of each appointments. The mobile client gets the scheduling data through a special web service (k-soap) which is not the part of the Enterprise software. The AE.Mobile Application is developed by me, together with my colleague Péter Dudás.
