Mitsubishi cars cyclic maintenance
After the course, I made an ASP.NET web application. The application can easily querying the periodical maintenance operations of Mitsubishi branded cars. The environment was .NET Framework v1.1 and the compiler was VS.Net 2003 at that time. I deploed the application under IIS. on live site (www.webmatrixhosting.com) in Moscow. It was working perfectly during the usage of hosting site was free, in mz case 6 months.
