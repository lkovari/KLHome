RPN. Calculator
My first project was a simple calculator which was written in Objective-C language. The calculator is an RPM. based (Reverse Polish Notation) calculator, which dissolve brackets in the arithmetic expression and the calculator engine, also contains an expression tree builder, which based on significant weighting of the arithmetic operations.
At that time I worked for my own company named EKLSoftTrade Llc. The company was the contractuated partner of the SIS. formerly the Amkai.
